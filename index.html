Abstract

A supplement to the classic rabbit epic Watership Down by Richard Adams. It includes a full index, a glossary, and chapter summaries.

Watership Down is the best book in the whole world. It was the basis for a smash hit 1978 film of the same name. I'm still recovering from that experience, as we all are.

Comments welcome: <johngtait@gmail.com>
